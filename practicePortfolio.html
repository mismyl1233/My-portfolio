About Me

Here are three things to know about me:

I currently live in Orlando, FL, but I have lived all over the USA: North Carolina, South Carolina, California, New Mexico, Georgia, and New York. I also lived in Costa Rica for a year when I was three years old, but I don't remember much about that! The pictures are amazing, however, and I would love to return for a visit.

I have worked in many career fields: restaurant industry, office manager for a thriving real estate company(I learned marketing and graphic design skills), online poker champion. I was also a jewelry maker and designer (the main focus was creating mala necklaces, but also custom gifts such as bridesmaid presents).

I was in a random accident four years ago that left me unable to walk without a cane, and not for long distances. This accident changed my life in so many ways! After trying to turn my passion (making beautiful jewelry) into a career, I have decided to study software engineering. I still love designing and making jewelry as a hobby, but the cost of materials/time spent did not equate into a viable pay. I also can't return to the restaurant industry because of my leg, but even though I enjoyed the challenging, fast-paced aspect of the job, and made many good friends, I never wanted to grow old/retire from that career. There is a silver lining to everything in life, and my accident has made me motivated and very strong.
